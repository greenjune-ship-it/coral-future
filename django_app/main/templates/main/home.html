CBASS System

A portable experimental system to run standardized short-term acute heat stress assays. CBASS assays allow determination of the ED50, the standardized temperature threshold at which 50% of initial photosystem efficiency is lost.

References:

  • Evensen et al. 2023
  • Voolstra et al. 2020
  • Voolstra et al. 2021
  • Savary et al. 2021
  • Evensen et al. 2021

R Package

R package to process CBASS-derived PAM data. Minimal requirements are PAM data (or data from any other continuous variable that changes with temperature, e.g. relative bleaching scores) from 4 samples (e.g., nubbins) subjected to 4 temperature profiles of at least 2 colonies from 1 coral species from 1 site.
